Find our West Cork Farm Tours on Ireland's southwest coastline - part of Ireland's Wild Atlantic Way touring route

And discover the hospitality offered in the charming villages and towns near Rosscarbery and Clonakilty.

Just an hour's drive from Cork city, you'll find yourself in rich countryside where farming and food production flourish in a climate shaped by the Atlantic.

Join us for a day tour, or immerse yourself in the West Cork experience by booking a tour package with one of our accommodation partners: the Celtic Ross Hotel in the coastal village of Rosscarbery and the Fernhill House Hotel and the Emmet Hotel in the award-winning town of Clonakilty: a European Destination of Excellence.

About Us

Avril & Willie Allshire - Willie, Avril, William and Maurice Allshire make a living where they love to live. With free-range outdoor pigs, forestry, an on-farm meat processing unit and timber processing unit, life is busy but they're proud of what they've achieved. Local, national and international awards benchmark their produce quality.

Michael & Marguerite Crowley - Michael, Marguerite and their 5 children run a dairy farm near Skibbereen, and their high-quality milk has won them acclaim as West Cork Dairy Farmers of the Year, Carbery Milk Quality Award Winners, and Winners at the NDC/Kerrygold National Milk Quality Awards.

John Joe O'Sullivan - John Joe O'Sullivan and his family run a dairy and calf-rearing enterprise near the picturesque West Cork village of Rosscarbery. The family have won Carbery Milk Quality and Origin Green Sustainability awards.

Denis O'Donovan, his wife Collette, and their son Eoghan farm on the Wild Atlantic Way between Rosscarbery and Glandore where their Jersey cross Friesian herd produce high solids of milk of exceptional quality from grazed grass.

Location

Farm Tour Locations

Rosscarbery and Clonakilty ar located on the N71 road which leads West from Cork city and Cork Airport. Bespoke tours can be arranged so that they begin from each of these locations or from locations in West Cork.

When you sign up for a scheduled weekly tour, we'll send you your exact starting point after you book.

Transport Links

Our West Cork Farm Tours begin just an hour's drive from Cork Airport which serves airports from Amsterdam, Barcelona, Birmingham and Bordeaux to Tenerife, Wroclaw and Zürich.
